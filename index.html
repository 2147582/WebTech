Difference of Internet and World Wide Web


The World Wide Web is NOT the Internet, and The Internet is NOT the World Wide Web

What is World Wide Web?

World Wide Web is a collection of documents and information interlinked by hyperlinks and Uniform Resource Identifier. World Wide Web allows users to retrieve and provide information. Pages are connected to other pages that are topic related using hyperlinks.
